# qaai_system — Institutional Trading Architecture

## Abstract
qaai_system is a hedge-fund-grade autonomous trading platform designed for
deterministic execution, governed intelligence, and institutional safety.

---

## System Pillars
1. Deterministic Data Pipeline
2. Strategy Lifecycle Governance
3. Tournament-Based Evidence
4. Paper → Live Promotion Discipline
5. Capital Safety & Kill Switches
6. Full Observability & Replay

---

## Execution Flow
Market Data → Screening → Watchlist → Strategy Candidates
→ Tournament → Paper Trading → Human Approval → Live Trading

---

## Intelligence Model
ML is used only for:
- Probability estimation
- Ranking
- Context enrichment

ML has **no execution authority**.

---

## Why qaai_system Is Different
- No black-box automation
- No silent state changes
- No ML-driven execution
- Full auditability

This design mirrors professional trading desks, not retail bots.

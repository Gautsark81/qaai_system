# Governance & Risk Doctrine

## Governance Model
- Human approval required for:
  - Live trading
  - Capital allocation
  - Parameter adaptation

---

## Risk Hierarchy
1. System Kill Switch
2. Capital Guard
3. Strategy Kill
4. Symbol Kill
5. Execution Layer

Higher levels override lower ones.

---

## Forbidden Actions
- Auto-promotion to live
- ML-driven overrides
- Hidden capital scaling

---

## Risk Philosophy
Survival > Consistency > Performance

A strategy that survives long enough will outperform one that doesn’t.

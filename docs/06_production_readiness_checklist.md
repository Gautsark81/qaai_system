# Production Readiness Checklist

## Environment
- Secrets isolated
- Paper vs Live separated
- Broker sandbox tested

---

## Safety
- Kill switches tested
- Capital caps enforced
- No auto-live paths

---

## Observability
- Events flowing
- Dashboard read-only
- Replay validated

---

## Governance
- Approval files in place
- Operator trained
- Incident runbooks ready

---

## Final Rule
If any item is unchecked → DO NOT GO LIVE

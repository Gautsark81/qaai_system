4 — Where the logic plugs into flow (sequence)

Signal generation (Indicator + ML) produces signals; Signal objects are published to SignalBus.

SignalScorer.score() is called by the signal consumer (in asset microservice) — signals with score < threshold are filtered or reduced in size.

AIRanker.suggest_weights(context) runs periodically (and on regime change) to update meta_controller weights; these influence PortfolioManager.net_orders.

ToggleAssistant.recommend() outputs per-asset toggles; governance.RuleEngine.evaluate() enforces them (blocks, cooldowns). Toggle changes are logged to ui/toggle_history and are visible in dashboard.

PortfolioManager calls governance and intelligence APIs before finalizing net plan.

Execution Engine receives plans, slices via VWAP/iceberg, reserves margin; governance/throttle enforces trade rate.

Monitoring collects metrics including toggle_changes_total, signal_score_histogram, ranker_recommendations, rule_engine_blocked_orders.

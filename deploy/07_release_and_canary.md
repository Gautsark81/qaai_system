# Release & Canary Strategy

## Release Types
- Patch (bug fix)
- Minor (feature)
- Major (architecture)

## Canary Rules
- All LIVE releases pass through LIVE-SANDBOX
- Capital capped to ≤5%
- Minimum observation: 3 trading days

## Promotion to Full LIVE
Requires:
- Zero critical incidents
- Operator sign-off
- Audit review
